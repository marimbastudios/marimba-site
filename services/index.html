Why this matters

Footage review is editorial work.
Not a free add-on.

Editorial work

Opening footage is the start of editing

Identifying usable takes, matching audio to video, renaming clips and organising a project structure all require opening every file, watching it back and making editorial decisions. That is post-production work — it requires time, judgment and skill. It is not included in a Shoot & Dump because it was not priced into a Shoot & Dump.

What a Shoot & Dump is

You pay for the camera, not the edit suite

A Shoot & Dump means: we show up, we film, we back everything up, we hand it over. The footage is yours as-captured. If you need anything done to it before it reaches your editor — review, naming, organisation — that is a separate scope of work. Choose Shoot & Organise, or add it as an optional line item.
